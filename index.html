A History of the Labradoodle

Labradoodles are really cool. At first, the Labradoodle was bred in Australia from the Labrador and Standard Poodle. The reason for such a breeding was to create a optimal guide dog. Labradoodles are also well-known for their hypoallergenic coats, which makes them a great alternative to other dogs for those who have allergies to dogs. They live up to 12 to 15 years on average, with smaller dogs often outliving the larger dogs of the same breed.
